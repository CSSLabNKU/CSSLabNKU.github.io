#### Selected Research Highlights

Our research has received more than 7,000 citations and has been covered by more than 200 media outlets worldwide. Representative publications have appeared in IEEE Symposium on Security and Privacy, USENIX Security, NDSS, ACM CCS, IEEE/IFIP DSN, ESORICS, IEEE TIFS, IEEE TDSC, IEEE TSC, and IEEE TII.

Recent and representative work includes:

- Yihe Duan, Ding Wang, and Yutong Li. **Credential Extraction Attacks Against Compromised Credential Checking Services of Password Managers.** IEEE Symposium on Security and Privacy, 2026, to appear.
- Yunkai Zou, Maoxiang An, and Ding Wang. **Password Guessing Using Large Language Models.** USENIX Security Symposium, 2025.
- Fei Duan, Ding Wang, and Chunfu Jia. **A Security Analysis of Honey Vaults.** IEEE Symposium on Security and Privacy, 2024.
- Ding Wang, Yunkai Zou, Yuan-An Xiao, Siqi Ma, and Xiaofeng Chen. **Pass2Edit: A Multi-Step Generative Model for Guessing Edited Passwords.** USENIX Security Symposium, 2023.
- Ding Wang, Yunkai Zou, Qiying Dong, Yuanming Song, and Xinyi Huang. **How to Attack and Generate Honeywords.** IEEE Symposium on Security and Privacy, 2022.
- Ding Wang, Ping Wang, Debiao He, and Yuan Tian. **Birthday, Name and Bifacial Security: Understanding Passwords of Chinese Web Users.** USENIX Security Symposium, 2019.
- Ding Wang, Haibo Cheng, Ping Wang, Jeff Yan, and Xinyi Huang. **A Security Analysis of Honeywords.** NDSS, 2018.
- Ding Wang, Haibo Cheng, Ping Wang, Xinyi Huang, and Gaopeng Jian. **Zipf's Law in Passwords.** IEEE Transactions on Information Forensics and Security, 2017.
